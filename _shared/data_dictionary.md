# Data Dictionary

Common variables used in synthetic examples:

- person_id: Synthetic person identifier.
- perceived_value: Perceived benefit or utility of an option.
- default_status: Whether an option is defaulted or preselected.
- salience_boost: Whether the choice environment increases visibility.
- friction: Effort, complexity, or hassle.
- trust: Perceived credibility of the institution or platform.
- social_norm_signal: Perceived behavior or approval of others.
- comprehension: Clarity and understanding of the option.
- probability_choose: Simulated probability of choosing an option.
- chose_option: Simulated binary choice outcome.
